Medical store system

Introduction:

Medical store system is the system in which we need to manage and 
analyse the medicines and arrange it accordingly.

Purpose:

Medical store system is to make the medicine management simple and 
easy.To make the record of medicines prices,sales and date.

Functional requirement of medical store system:

To check the avalabelity medical stock
To check the expiry dates of medical
Arranging it in orderwise 
Updating the prices in record
Recording the sales and bills

Non functional requirements:
Making easy to take any medicines.
Make management simple and easy.
Keeping tracks on dates and expiry dates.
Make easy and fast for payment.

Characteristics:

Quick accessebality.
Secure management.
Updates and security.

 Acceptance crateria:

Given medicine gets fastly and quickly.
The medicines should not be expired.
The medicines should be kept in arranged order.
Avalabilty of stocks.
Quick accessebility.
